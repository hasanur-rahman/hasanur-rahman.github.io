Md Hasanur Rahman

PhD Candidate
Computer Science, University of Iowa
 


I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science at the University of Iowa, where I am fortunate to be advised by Dr. Guanpeng Li. Currently in the fourth year of my PhD, I am a member of the Dependable System Lab at the University of Iowa. I am also a research intern at Argonne National Laboratory, supervised by Dr. Sheng Di. Before beginning my doctoral studies, I spent two years (2019–2021) working at Samsung Research, Bangladesh. Prior to that, I earned my undergraduate degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (2015-2019).
